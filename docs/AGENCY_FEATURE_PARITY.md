# Agency Feature Parity — Complete ✅

## Overview
Agencies are now **feature-complete** with Venues and Personnel. All revenue-critical features have been implemented.

---

## ✅ New Features Added (7 total)

### 1. **Mission Control** (`/d/agency/mission-control`)
- Real-time chat hub for managing staff during shifts
- Automatic chat creation when staff are assigned to bookings
- Quick actions: arriving, on-site, position, break, leaving, location sharing
- Call buttons for each team member
- Identical to venue Mission Control but with agency branding

**Why it matters:** Agencies need to coordinate multiple staff across multiple venues simultaneously.

---

### 2. **AI Assistant** (`/d/agency/ai`)
- Access to Shield AI for legal reports, incident analysis, staffing guidance
- Document upload and reference
- Conversation history
- SIA licensing and compliance knowledge

**Why it matters:** Agencies handle complex compliance and legal issues across many clients.

---

### 3. **Live Tracking** (`/d/agency/live`)
- Real-time GPS tracking of all staff across active shifts
- Map view with staff locations
- Check-in/check-out status

**Why it matters:** Agencies need visibility across all their deployed staff, not just one venue.

---

### 4. **Incidents** (`/d/agency/incidents`)
- View all incident reports submitted by agency staff
- Filter by venue, staff member, date
- Export for compliance

**Why it matters:** Agencies are liable for incidents involving their staff and need centralized reporting.

---

### 5. **Staff Ratings** (`/d/agency/ratings`)
- View venue feedback and ratings for each staff member
- Performance tracking
- Identify top performers and areas for improvement

**Why it matters:** Agencies need to manage staff quality and match the right people to the right venues.

---

### 6. **Preferred Venues** (`/d/agency/preferred-venues`)
- See which venues book your agency most frequently
- Total bookings, last booking date, average rating
- Quick access to venue profiles

**Why it matters:** Agencies build long-term relationships with key venue clients — this helps prioritize and nurture those relationships.

---

### 7. **Revenue Analytics** (`/d/agency/revenue`)
- Total revenue, monthly revenue, growth %
- Revenue by venue (top 10)
- Revenue over time (line chart)
- Time range filters (month, quarter, year)

**Why it matters:** Agencies are businesses — revenue tracking is essential for growth and forecasting.
