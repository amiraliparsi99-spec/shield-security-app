# Shield Demo Video Script

**Duration:** 90-120 seconds  
**Tone:** Professional but approachable, energetic but not salesy  
**Target:** Venue owners/managers in Birmingham

---

## SCENE 1: The Problem (0:00 - 0:20)

**[Visual: Split screen - frantic manager on phone / empty door position at venue]**

**Voiceover:**
> "It's Friday afternoon. Your door supervisor just called in sick. You've got 3 hours until doors open and no backup. Sound familiar?"

**[Visual: Clock ticking, phone calls going to voicemail]**

> "Calling agencies. Leaving voicemails. Hoping someone calls back in time."

---

## SCENE 2: Introduce Shield (0:20 - 0:35)

**[Visual: Shield app opening on phone, clean interface]**

**Voiceover:**
> "There's a better way. Meet Shield — the app that connects your venue with verified security professionals in minutes, not hours."

**[Visual: App logo animation]**

---

## SCENE 3: How It Works (0:35 - 1:05)

**[Visual: Screen recording of app workflow]**

**Voiceover:**
> "Here's how simple it is."

**[Visual: Posting a shift - tapping through the form]**

> "Step one: Post your shift. Tonight, 9pm to 3am, need 2 door supervisors. Done in 30 seconds."

**[Visual: Matches appearing - profiles with ratings, SIA badges]**

> "Step two: See who's available. Real profiles. Real ratings from other Birmingham venues. Verified SIA licenses."

**[Visual: Tapping to book, confirmation screen]**

> "Step three: Book with one tap. They get notified instantly. You get confirmation. Sorted."

---

## SCENE 4: Trust & Verification (1:05 - 1:20)

**[Visual: SIA license verification badge, 5-star reviews]**

**Voiceover:**
> "Every professional on Shield has a verified SIA license. You see their ratings, their experience, their specialties. No more gambling on who shows up."

---

## SCENE 5: Pricing (1:20 - 1:30)

**[Visual: Simple pricing graphic - £0 + 10%]**

**Voiceover:**
> "And the best part? It's free to join. You only pay a small platform fee when you actually book. No contracts. No monthly fees."

---

## SCENE 6: Call to Action (1:30 - 1:45)

**[Visual: App download screen / QR code / URL]**

**Voiceover:**
> "Shield. Security staffing that actually works. Sign up free at shieldapp.co.uk"

**[Visual: Logo + tagline]**

> "Post your first shift in under 5 minutes."

---

## B-ROLL SUGGESTIONS

- Birmingham city centre nightlife (Broad Street, Digbeth)
- Professional door staff at work (get permission!)
- Venue manager using phone/tablet
- Clock/time imagery for urgency
- Shield app interface (screen recordings)

## MUSIC SUGGESTIONS

- Modern, upbeat but not cheesy
- Electronic/corporate hybrid
- Builds energy during the demo section
- Resolves satisfyingly at CTA

## GRAPHICS NEEDED

1. Shield logo animation
2. "Verified SIA" badge graphic  
3. Star rating display
4. Simple pricing infographic (£0 + 10%)
5. QR code linking to signup
6. End card with logo + URL

---

## RECORDING TIPS

1. Use real app footage, not mockups
2. Show Birmingham-specific elements if possible
3. Keep text on screen minimal
4. Use captions (most social video is watched muted)
5. Make the first 3 seconds attention-grabbing

---

- Landing page (embedded)

- Instagram Reels / TikTok (60-sec version)
- Facebook (for local Birmingham targeting)
